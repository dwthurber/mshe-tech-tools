Academic Affairs and Faculty Development

  • What unique academic challenges confront community college students who work full-time, and how can institutions provide academic support?
  • How are faculty supported to ensure quality in online distance education, and how does the administration communicate this support?
  • What do PhD students and graduate faculty identify as the motivations for and perceived value of pursuing PhD study in humanities disciplines? 
  • What factors most strongly influence Millennials to major in the liberal arts and sciences at a highly selective research university?

Admissions and Enrollment Management

  • What is the evidence that test-optional admission policies broaden access to selective colleges for students from diverse social and economic backgrounds?
  • How do U.S. News & World Report rankings affect college admissions?
  • How does the process of completing college application essays reflect or conflict with adolescent cognitive and identity development?
  • How engaged are transfer students in the campus community, and how do formal transition resources shape their engagement?

Alumni Relations and Development

  • How does the use of online social networking sites by development departments relate to the giving of young alumni?
  • How are new patterns in philanthropy changing gifting to higher education institutions?

Athletics

  • How do African American male college athletes experience racial positional segregation (“stacking”), and how does this phenomenon affect them?
  • How can the athletic department at a private, selective, Division I university facilitate career development for student athletes?
  • Do institutional differences such as size, type and athletic operating budget affect NCAA Division I institutions’ Title IX compliance decisions?

Career Services

  • What sources of information do liberal arts and sciences graduate students use to answer career questions?
  • How can University Career Services at Northwestern effectively meet the career needs of international undergraduate students?
  • What do Millennial MBAs expect of employers, and how have these expectations changed since the economic downturn?

Diversity and Access

  • In what ways do lesbian, gay or bisexual Christian students express and reconcile their sexual identities and Christian identities?
  • What services and accommodations successfully meet the needs of ADHD students at private universities?
  • How do first-generation students at Northwestern University develop resource networks, and how do these networks contribute to their success on campus?

Financial Issues

  • How are large four-year public universities responding to demands to provide financial literacy education, and what approaches are proving 
  • most effective?
  • How can traditional liberal arts colleges adjust their business models to maintain financial stability?
  • How does a student’s perception of undergraduate loan debt affect major life choices?

Higher Education Policy

  • What are the intended and unintended effects of changes in city alcohol ordinances on students, the university and the city where the university is located?
  • How has the increased availability and procurement of academic earmarks affected an institution’s relationship with its primary education association?
  • What are the characteristics of open access policies adopted at universities in the US, and what lessons can be learned from institutions that have adopted these policies?

Strategic Planning and Institutional Research

  • What are the factors influencing faculty engagement in scholarship at a low-tiered research university, and how do institutional policy and strategic planning affect research productivity?
  • How can understanding administrators’ perceptions of institutional culture and goals motivate and facilitate engagement with their division’s strategic plan?
  • How do Chicago-area colleges and universities use their wealth and donative resources when creating tuition pricing, institutional aid and educational cost strategies? 

Student Development

  • How does freshmen participation in a wilderness pre-orientation program at Northwestern 
  • University foster student development and impact campus involvement throughout the next four years of college?
  • Do institutions have an obligation to address the moral and ethical development of students outside the classroom by creating conduct codes?
  • What are the differences in the levels of engagement and institutional satisfaction of community college veteran students who participate in student veteran organizations and those who do not?

Student Wellness

  • What social factors influence first-year women in four-year colleges and universities to drink dangerously?
  • What makes the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign’s suicide prevention program successful?
  • How has a private university combined alcohol and sexual assault education, prevention and response programming, and which approaches are proving most effective?

Study Abroad and International Education

  • What are the impacts on global awareness for community college students who study abroad?
  • How does study abroad outside of Israel affect the Jewish engagement of Jewish college students?
  • How do traditional study abroad programs compare with service learning abroad programs in regards to the impact on the anticipated career paths and educational plans of participants?
  • What are the academic and social expectations of international undergraduate students entering a US university, and how are these expectations transformed within the first year of attendance?
  • How does study abroad impact the racial, ethnic and social identity development of Asian American undergraduate students?
